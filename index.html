Our Journey

We are GOATS Robotics

A FIRST Robotics High School school based team, purposed to compete in the FTC Relic Recovery Challenge.
We are all students that attend Evergreen Valley High School in San Jose, California.

What have we done?

Shattered Expectations

After back-to-back seasons of performing as one of the worst teams at the tournament, our team decided that we needed to approach our system and team differently.

This year, we came back as ready as ever and were focused on both aspects of FIRST: bettering our community and learning the beauty of robotics.

Won Awards

We participated at two tournaments this season: the San Jose Qualifiers #2 and the Folsom Qualifiers #2. We have won the Motivate Award, Finalist Award (San Jose #2), and the Winning Alliance Award (Folsom #2).
